The Surgeon's Profile

Dr. Sandeep Boddeda is an experienced Orthopedic Surgeon with over five years of dedicated expertise in complex Joint Replacement and Trauma Surgeries.

He completed his MBBS from the prestigious Andhra Medical College and pursued his MS in Orthopaedics at Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences (NIMS). Further specializing in joint replacement, he completed a fellowship at Sunshine Hospitals, gaining advanced training in hip and knee arthroplasty.

A MAKO-certified Robotic Joint Replacement Surgeon, he has successfully performed over 1,000 joint replacement surgeries and 400 trauma surgeries in the last five years. His precision and expertise ensure optimal outcomes for his patients, focused on restoring mobility and improving the quality of life.
